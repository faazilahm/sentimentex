sentimentex

Inspired by those with Asperger's, sentimentex is the idea of Siyuan Liu, Faazliah Mohamed, and Saniyah Shaikh at PennApps xvi (Fall 2017). It aims to help those who have a hard time discerning the emotions from texts, emails, and other text-based media by using machine learning, natural language processing, and webscraping to calculate the percentages of emotions present in the given text.
