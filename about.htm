sentimentex

Inspired by those with Asperger's, sentimentex is the idea of Siyuan Liu, Faazliah Mohamed, and Saniyah Shaikh at PennApps Fall 2017. It aims to help those who have hard times discerning the emotions from texts, emails, and other text-based media by using machine learning to calculate the percentages of emotions present in the given text. sentimentex also provides suggestions for responses to further increase accessibility.
